# Privacy Policy

## Analytics

Sky Map uses **Firebase Analytics** (provided by Google) to help us understand how people
use the app. This applies only to the Google Play Store version of Sky Map. The F-Droid
version collects no analytics data whatsoever. We use this information to improve the app and no
other purpose. The data is aggregated and anonymized by Google before we receive it. Even so,
we do not share the data with any third parties.


### What we collect

Firebase Analytics collects the following information anonymously:

- **Usage patterns**: when you launch the app (hour of day, day of week), how long each
  session lasts, and which features you open (gallery, time travel, settings, etc.)
- **Search terms**: the names of objects you search for, and whether the search succeeded.
  Searches are unlikely to contain personal information, but you should be aware that the
  full search term is logged.
- **Feature interactions**: which sky layers you toggle, which time travel events you select,
  which gallery images you view, and whether you use manual or automatic sensor mode.
- **Device capabilities**: which motion sensors your device has (accelerometer, gyroscope,
  magnetometer, rotation vector) and which sensor path is active. This helps us understand
  which hardware to support.
- **Settings snapshot**: key preference values at each session start (e.g. whether gyro is
  disabled, sensor speed/damping, night mode state). This helps us understand which settings
  people actually use.
- **Language**: your device locale (e.g. `en-US`) to help us prioritise translations.
- **Install version**: for new installs only, the app version you first installed. This helps
  us understand user retention across releases.

### What we do NOT collect

- **Location**: your location (whether entered manually or from GPS) is never sent to
  analytics. Location preference fields are explicitly excluded.
- **Any other personally identifiable information**: we cannot identify you as an individual.

### How the data is used

The anonymous data is transmitted to Google (not the Sky Map developers directly). Firebase
Analytics compiles statistical reports that tell us things like which features are popular,
which device capabilities are common, and how session length varies across users. This helps
us decide which features to improve, which languages to support, and when to drop support for
obsolete hardware.

### Opting out

We respect your privacy and do not collect your personal information - we cannot tell anything
about you as a user specifically.

If you do not wish to share analytics data, you can opt out at any time via **Settings →
Enable analytics**. Disabling analytics suppresses all data collection, including Firebase's
own automatic events.

Otherwise, thank you for helping make Sky Map better.

For more information on Firebase Analytics see https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy.
