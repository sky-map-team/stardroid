<b>The original sky map, kept simple.</b>
Sky Map remains the cleanest way to explore the stars. Originally built by
Google and now powered by the community, it provides a fast, ad-free experience
that focuses on what matters: the sky.

<h3>Key Features:</h3>
⭐ <b>Automatic Tracking:/b> Just point and see.
⭐ <b>Expanded Knowledge:/b> Use the new info cards to get data on stars, planets, and nebulae.
⭐ <b>Small App Size:/b> Doesn’t hog your storage or slow down your phone.
⭐ <b>Open Source:/b> A community-driven project dedicated to free astronomy for all.
