<b>Turn your phone into a window to the universe.</b>
Sky Map makes stargazing easy for everyone. Whether you’re camping with
family or just in your backyard, simply hold up your device to see the
constellations and planets hidden in plain sight.

<h3>Simple & Educational:</h3>
⭐ <b>Interactive Star Map:</b> The display moves as you move, showing you the sky in real-time.
⭐ <b>Discover the Details:</b> With our new info cards, you can learn facts about the objects you find.
⭐ <b>Lightweight & Fast:</b> Starts instantly and works without a data connection in the middle of nowhere.
⭐ <b>Ad-Free Forever: Enjoy the view without distractions or pop-ups.
