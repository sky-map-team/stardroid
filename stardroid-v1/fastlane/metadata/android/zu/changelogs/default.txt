<b><font color="#F67E81">Wamkelekile</font></b> Isipiliyoni esisha se-onboarding sisiza abasebenzisi abangabazali ukuba baqalise ngokushesha.
<b><font color="#F67E81">Indawo</font></b> Ukuphathwa kwendawo okwenziwe kabusha ngesimo esicacile, izinkomba zeprogress, kanye nemephu entsha.
<b><font color="#F67E81">Sesha</font></b> Sesha nge-RA/Dec coordinates noma ngegama.
